ABOUT US
iClARE is a digital healthcare consultation services app, also known as telemedicine. This is an online platform designed to offer remote healthcare services. Through these services, patients can consult with doctors and healthcare providers through video calls, live chat, phone calls, or text inquiries.

This app is aimed at addressing barriers to healthcare access, such as geographic distance, cost, and the inconvenience of in-person visits. With a subscription-based model, it seeks to offer consumers a trustworthy and reasonably priced healthcare substitute.

Services
The app aims to democratize access to healthcare services through virtual consultations, appointment scheduling, and ongoing health monitoring.
Virtual Consultation
Get professional health advice from the comfort of your home. Schedule online consultations with certified health professionals through personalized Zoom links for a convenient and tailored healthcare experience.
Health Monitoring
Stay on top of your health with our monthly checkup services. Our consistent monitoring ensures early detection, preventive care, and continuous improvement of your overall well-being.
Mental Health
Your mental health matters. Access counseling and therapy services provided by experienced professionals, designed to help you navigate challenges and foster emotional resilience in a safe and supportive environment.
Record Access
Easily request and access your medical documents when you need them. Our system ensures that your health records are organized, secure, and readily available for your convenience.
